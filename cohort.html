Six-week schedule

What happens each week.

90 minutes live per week. 2 to 3 hours of self-paced curriculum work between sessions. Outcomes-focused from day one.

Week 01

Orientation and Career Targeting

Cohort introductions, your specific career target, and the 6-week plan. Live walkthrough of the Career Track Decision Tree applied to your situation.

Week 02

Resume and LinkedIn Workshop

Live workshop using the templates. Your personalized written resume review is delivered this week with track-changes annotations.

Week 03

Mock Interview Round 1: Behavioral

Live mock behavioral interview during the session. Peer feedback, instructor critique, written follow-up notes.

Week 04

Mock Interview Round 2: Technical

Live mock technical interview using scenario questions from the Course. Live calibration on what hiring managers actually look for.

Week 05

Salary Negotiation Roleplay

Live roleplay using the Salary Negotiation Playbook scripts. Plus a hiring manager guest session with live Q&A.

Week 06

Launch and Network

Final positioning review, action plan for the next 30 days, and onboarding into the alumni community. One hiring contact intro initiated for those with a fit.

Common questions

Cohort FAQ.

What is the time commitment?

90 minutes live per week for six weeks, plus 2 to 3 hours of self-paced curriculum work between sessions. Total commitment is roughly 4 to 5 hours per week for six weeks. Sessions are recorded if you miss a week.

What if I miss a session?

Sessions are recorded and posted within 24 hours. Personalized deliverables (resume review, mock interview feedback) are scheduled around your availability. Slack participation continues regardless of live attendance.

Why is there an application?

Cohort fit matters. The application is short and exists so that everyone in the room is at roughly the same career stage and committed to active job search within 90 days. If you are not a fit, you are not accepted, and there is no charge.

How is this different from the Complete Course?

The Course is on-demand with lifetime access. The Cohort adds six weeks of live instruction, a personalized written resume review, two live mock interviews, a LinkedIn audit, salary negotiation drills, hiring manager guest sessions, and weekly job lead drops. The Cohort includes everything in the Course. Pick the Cohort if you want personal feedback and accountability.

Can I get my company to pay?

Yes. A customizable employer justification letter is included. It outlines the curriculum, deliverables, and ROI in language designed for HR and learning and development budgets. Many regulated companies have annual professional development budgets that cover this.

What is the refund policy?

Full refund if your application is not accepted. Once accepted and enrolled, refunds are available up through the end of week one. After week one, no refunds, but you can defer to a future cohort if a hardship arises.
