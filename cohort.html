Six weeks. One launch.

Each week pairs live group coaching with structured deliverables. By the end, you have a resume, a job pipeline, and interview reps that put you ahead of 90% of candidates.

WEEK01

Foundations & Positioning

Define your target track, your story, and your positioning. Group session covers the four major tracks and how to identify the right fit for your background.

WEEK02

Resume & LinkedIn Build

Live resume teardowns. Each student gets feedback on their draft. By end of week, you have a resume that hiring managers in this industry recognize.

WEEK03

The Job Search Engine

Build your target list, your outreach scripts, and your weekly pipeline cadence. Live demo of how to find roles that aren't on the major job boards.

WEEK04

Technical Interview Prep

The technical questions that come up in Regulatory, Quality, and Clinical interviews. Live mock interview reps with feedback in real time.

WEEK05

Behavioral & Leadership Interviews

The STAR framework adapted for Regulated Industries. How to talk about technical work in plain English and how to handle the curveball questions.

WEEK06

Negotiation & Launch Plan

Salary research, offer evaluation, and live negotiation drills. You leave the cohort with a 30, 60, and 90-day plan for your first role.

COMMON QUESTIONS

Before you apply.

When does the next cohort start?

Cohorts run quarterly. The next start date will be confirmed when you receive your application response. We email accepted applicants two to three weeks before the cohort begins so you have time to prepare.

What happens if I can't make a live session?

Every session is recorded and posted to the cohort community within 24 hours. We do recommend attending at least four of the six sessions live to get the full benefit of the live coaching format.

Why is the application required?

Cohorts are capped at 20 students to keep the experience personal and high-touch. The application is how we ensure each student is the right fit and that the group is calibrated for productive peer learning. It also gives Brice context to personalize feedback during the cohort.

Is the course a prerequisite for the cohort?

No, but many cohort students have completed the Break Into Regulated Industries course beforehand. The course gives you the foundation; the cohort applies it live with feedback. Both can stand alone, but they reinforce each other.

What's the refund policy?

Cohort enrollment is non-refundable once the cohort begins. If you need to defer to a later cohort due to a major life event, reach out and we will work with you in good faith.
