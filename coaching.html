Common questions

Coaching FAQ.

Why is this application only?

Two reasons. First, a 1:1 engagement at this level only works when the fit is real. The application process surfaces fit before either side commits. Second, capacity is limited. Active engagements are capped to protect the quality of the work.

Can I do less than three months?

No. Three months is the minimum because meaningful career movement at the executive level rarely happens in less time. The structure assumes a 90-day arc with weekly progression.

What if I want to pause or extend?

Pauses are accommodated for hardship, family events, or work travel without penalty. Extensions beyond the initial 90 days are common and offered at the same rate. Most engagements continue past the minimum.

How does Slack messaging work?

You join a private Slack channel. Monday through Friday, you can message at any time. Responses typically within a few hours during business hours, sometimes faster. Used for quick questions, document reviews, real-time negotiation support, and async strategy.

What about confidentiality?

Engagement details, conversations, and any documents shared are confidential. The relationship is structured as advisor and advisee. Nothing said in the engagement is shared externally without explicit permission.

Can my employer pay?

Yes. Many senior engagements are funded through professional development budgets or executive coaching budgets. A justification document is provided upon request to support the conversation with your finance or HR team.

What if I am not accepted?

You receive a clear note explaining the fit gap and a recommendation for the right next step. That might be the Cohort, the Course, or another resource. No charge if not accepted.

What is the refund policy?

Full refund if not accepted. Once engagement begins, the three-month commitment is non-refundable but pause-able for documented hardship. Paid-in-full clients receive the same refund protection on the unaccepted scenario.
