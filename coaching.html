Common questions

Coaching FAQ.

Why is there an intake form before I can purchase?

The intake form takes about 5 minutes and helps me prepare for our first session. Instead of spending hour one getting to know your background, role, and goals, we spend it on actual strategy. You will be sent to your selected Stripe payment option immediately after submitting.

How long should I commit to coaching?

Three months is the recommended arc because meaningful career movement at the executive level rarely happens in less time. The Paid in Full option is built around a 90-day engagement and saves you $1,000 versus the monthly rate. That said, you can subscribe monthly and cancel anytime if your situation changes. Most clients who see real results stay engaged for at least three months.

What if I want to pause or extend?

Monthly clients can pause or cancel anytime by managing their subscription. Pauses are accommodated for hardship, family events, or work travel without penalty. Most engagements continue past the recommended three-month arc as clients see real career momentum building.

How does Slack messaging work?

You join a private Slack channel. Monday through Friday, you can message at any time. Responses typically within a few hours during business hours, sometimes faster. Used for quick questions, document reviews, real-time negotiation support, and async strategy.

What about confidentiality?

Engagement details, conversations, and any documents shared are confidential. The relationship is structured as advisor and advisee. Nothing said in the engagement is shared externally without explicit permission.

Can my employer pay?

Yes. Many senior engagements are funded through professional development budgets or executive coaching budgets. A justification document is provided upon request to support the conversation with your finance or HR team.

What if I am not sure which pricing option is right for me?

Pick "Not sure yet" on the intake form and you will be directed to a quick scheduling page to chat through fit and pricing before any payment is made. No pressure, no charge for the conversation.

What is the refund policy?

Monthly clients can cancel anytime through their Stripe customer portal. No further charges after cancellation. Past months are non-refundable since coaching has already been delivered. Paid-in-Full clients receive a prorated refund within the first 14 days if the engagement is not the right fit.
