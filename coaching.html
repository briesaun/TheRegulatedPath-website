FIT CRITERIA

Honest about who this is for.

Private coaching is a serious investment of time and money. Here's the honest read on who this works for and who it doesn't.

This is for you if...

  • You are committed to making a move in the next 3 to 6 months
  • You can show up to weekly sessions and do the work between them
  • You want senior-level career strategy, not just resume tips
  • You value speed and direct feedback over hand-holding
  • The cost of staying where you are is higher than the cost of coaching

This is not for you if...

  • You are still trying to decide if you want this career path
  • You aren't ready to commit to weekly work over three months
  • You want passive content, not active coaching
  • You haven't completed The Handbook or the Course yet
  • You aren't ready for direct, sometimes uncomfortable feedback

COMMON QUESTIONS

Before you apply.

Why a three-month minimum?

Real career moves take time. The first month is positioning and pipeline building. The second month is interviews and feedback cycles. The third month is closing offers and negotiation. Anything shorter than this is unlikely to produce the outcomes coaching exists to create.

What's the format of weekly sessions?

60 minutes via video, same time slot weekly so it's predictable. Sessions are recorded for your reference. Between sessions, you get direct messaging access for quick questions, document reviews, and real-time situations like an offer that just came in.

Do I need to complete the Course before applying?

It's not required, but it's strongly preferred. Coaching is most effective when you already have the foundational knowledge from The Handbook and the Course. If you're brand new to Regulated Industries, the smarter move is to start there. If you apply and the fit isn't right yet, I'll tell you.

How is this different from the cohort?

The cohort is a group of 20 working through a structured six-week curriculum together. Coaching is fully customized to your situation, your industry track, your target companies, and your specific timeline. Coaching is the right call when your move is high-stakes enough to warrant senior, individualized attention.

Can I cancel after the three-month minimum?

Yes. After the initial three months, the engagement converts to month-to-month. You can continue, pause, or end at any time with two weeks' notice.

What if I'm not accepted?

If we're not a fit right now, you'll receive a personal response with what to focus on first. In most cases, the right next step is The Handbook or the Course. You can reapply once you've completed those and you're ready for the level of intensity coaching requires.
