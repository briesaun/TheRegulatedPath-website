Do I need a science degree to break into regulated industries?

No. Regulatory Affairs, Quality Assurance, and Regulatory Operations roles hire from a wide range of backgrounds including communications, business, English, and information management. The Handbook walks you through how to position your existing experience using the right vocabulary.

You get an instant-download PDF immediately after purchase, plus separate downloadable files for the resume templates, LinkedIn templates, email scripts, glossary, and Job Tracker Template (Notion and Excel versions). Everything is sent to your email and accessible from your account.
