Common questions

What you should know before you enroll.

Does RI mean Regulated Industries?

Yes. Throughout the site, RI stands for Regulated Industries. That covers medical devices, pharmaceuticals, biotech, diagnostics, combination products, and the supporting Quality, Clinical, and Regulatory functions across them.

Is the Knowledge Trainer App available on its own?

No. The Knowledge Trainer App is exclusively bundled with the Break Into Regulated Industries course. Every paid student of the course gets full app access as part of the program. There is no standalone subscription and no free tier.

Do I need a science background to enroll?

A science, engineering, or healthcare background helps but is not required. The course is designed to bring smart career-changers up to speed on the language and frameworks of Regulated Industries. What matters more is your willingness to learn the technical terminology and your readiness to interview seriously.

How is the cohort different from the self-paced course?

The course is asynchronous video and materials you can work through anytime. The cohort is six weeks of live group sessions where you get direct feedback on your resume, mock interview reps, salary negotiation drills, and accountability from a small group of peers all moving toward the same goal. The cohort is for people who learn faster with structure and pressure.

What is private coaching for?

Private coaching is for candidates who are already in motion and want a senior practitioner in their corner week over week. We work on your specific positioning, your specific target companies, and your specific offers. It is application-only because not every candidate is the right fit and I cap the number of clients I take.

Can I get a refund?

The Handbook and the Course are non-refundable once accessed because of the digital nature of the materials. Cohort and coaching engagements have specific terms outlined in your enrollment agreement.
