COMMON QUESTIONS

Before you enroll.

How long does the course take?

The eight modules total roughly 12 to 15 hours of video. Most students complete the curriculum in three to six weeks at a pace of two to three modules per week. You have lifetime access, so you can move at the speed that fits your life.

Do I get the Knowledge Trainer App for free?

The Knowledge Trainer App is bundled into your enrollment. You receive access immediately after purchase. There is no separate subscription and no additional cost. The app is exclusive to Break Into RI students.

Do I need a science background to enroll?

A science, engineering, or healthcare background helps but is not required. The course is designed to bring smart career-changers up to speed on the technical language of Regulated Industries. What matters most is your commitment to learn the terminology and your readiness to interview seriously.

What's the difference between this course and the cohort?

The course is asynchronous video and materials you can work through anytime. The six-week cohort layers live group sessions on top with resume reviews, mock interviews, and accountability. Many students do the course first, then join the cohort for the live application of what they learned.

Is there a refund policy?

Because the materials are digital and access is granted immediately, the course is non-refundable once accessed. If you have questions before enrolling, reach out and ask.
